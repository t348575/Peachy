Our Poll's can be used to conduct survey's, and determine popularity of given choices. Advanced statistics such as number of visits, locations and so on can also be monitored.

Step's to create a poll:

1. Login to your peachy account.

2. Enter the number of options / choices.

3. Enter a final date or time until which people can vote.

4. Add pictures ( optional ) from either local device or a hyperlink.

5. Share generated link and enjoy!
